常见问题

我的记录存在哪里？

默认情况下，饮品记录、照片贴纸和偏好设置保存在你的设备本地。吨吨不会将这些内容上传到开发者服务器。

可以使用 iCloud 吗？

可以。启用 iCloud 同步后，数据会通过你的个人 iCloud 账户在支持的设备之间同步。开发者无法访问你的 iCloud 内容。

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are my records stored?

By default, your drink records, photo stickers, and preferences are stored locally on your device. DunDun does not upload this content to a developer-operated server.

Can I use iCloud?

Yes. When iCloud sync is enabled, data can sync across supported devices through your personal iCloud account. The developer cannot access your iCloud content.

照片与相机

为什么需要相机权限？

只有当你主动拍摄饮品照片时，吨吨才会请求相机权限。

为什么需要相册权限？

只有当你选择从相册上传照片时，吨吨才会请求相册访问权限。你可以仅允许访问选中的照片，并可在 iPhone 设置中随时修改权限范围。

Photos and Camera

Why does DunDun request camera access?

DunDun requests camera access only when you choose to take a drink photo.

Why does DunDun request photo access?

DunDun requests photo access only when you upload a photo. You can allow access to selected photos only and change the permission at any time in iPhone Settings.

删除与备份

如何删除一条记录？

在 App 内打开对应记录并选择删除。启用 iCloud 同步后，删除操作会同步到其他设备。

卸载 App 会删除全部内容吗？

本地数据会随 App 卸载而移除。如果启用了 iCloud，同步数据可能仍保存在你的 iCloud 中。

Deletion and Backup

How do I delete a record?

Open the relevant record in the app and choose delete. When iCloud sync is enabled, deletion is synchronized across devices.

Does uninstalling the app delete everything?

Local data is removed when the app is uninstalled. If iCloud sync is enabled, synchronized data may remain in your iCloud account.
