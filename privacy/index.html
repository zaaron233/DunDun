DunDun Privacy Policy

DunDun uses local storage by default and minimizes access to personal information. Your drink records belong to you.

生效日期：2026 年 6 月 5 日

概述

本隐私政策适用于吨吨（DunDun，副标题：Track Every Sip）。吨吨用于记录饮品、饮品照片、偏好及相关信息。

吨吨不会要求创建账户。默认情况下，数据仅保存在你的设备本地。你可以自行选择是否启用 iCloud 同步。

Effective date: June 5, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to DunDun ("Track Every Sip"). DunDun helps you record drinks, drink photos, preferences, and related information.

DunDun does not require account creation. By default, data is stored locally on your device. You can choose whether to enable iCloud sync.

1. 本地存储的数据

根据你使用的功能，吨吨可能在设备本地保存：

  • 饮品记录，包括品牌、饮品名称、时间、价格、口味偏好和备注
  • 你主动拍摄或上传的饮品照片，以及由照片生成的贴纸
  • 收藏、成就、偏好设置和 App 配置

这些数据不会被上传至开发者运营的服务器。

1. Data stored locally

Depending on the features you use, DunDun may store the following locally on your device:

  • Drink records, including brand, drink name, time, price, taste preferences, and notes
  • Drink photos you choose to take or upload and stickers generated from those photos
  • Favorites, achievements, preferences, and app configuration

This data is not uploaded to a developer-operated server.

2. 可选 iCloud 同步

你可以选择使用 iCloud 保存和同步数据。启用后，数据会通过你的个人 iCloud 账户在支持的 Apple 设备之间同步。

开发者不会运营独立云端数据库，也无法访问你个人 iCloud 账户中的内容。iCloud 服务由 Apple 提供，并受 Apple 相关条款约束。

2. Optional iCloud sync

You can choose to save and synchronize data with iCloud. If enabled, data is synchronized across supported Apple devices through your personal iCloud account.

The developer does not operate a separate cloud database and cannot access content in your personal iCloud account. iCloud is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's applicable terms.

3. 相机与照片权限

当你主动拍摄饮品照片时，吨吨会请求相机权限。

当你主动上传照片时，吨吨会请求照片访问权限。你可以选择仅允许访问特定照片，也可以在设备设置中随时调整或撤销权限。

吨吨不会在未经你操作的情况下读取、扫描或上传照片。

3. Camera and photo permissions

DunDun requests camera access when you choose to take a drink photo.

DunDun requests photo access when you upload a photo. You can allow access to selected photos only and change or revoke this permission at any time in device settings.

DunDun does not read, scan, or upload your photos without an action initiated by you.

4. 数据收集、分享与追踪

吨吨不会将你的个人数据发送给开发者，不会出售或出租数据，也不会与广告商分享数据。

吨吨不使用广告追踪，也不会跨其他公司的 App 或网站追踪你。

4. Data collection, sharing, and tracking

DunDun does not send your personal data to the developer, sell or rent your data, or share your data with advertisers.

DunDun does not use advertising tracking and does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies.

5. 删除数据

你可以在 App 内删除记录。启用 iCloud 同步后，删除操作会同步至其他设备。

卸载 App 会移除设备上的本地数据。如果启用了 iCloud，同步数据可能仍保存在你的 iCloud 账户中。你可以通过设备的 iCloud 设置管理相关数据。

5. Data deletion

You can delete records within the app. When iCloud sync is enabled, deletions are synchronized across devices.

Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data from your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, synchronized data may remain in your iCloud account. You can manage related data through your device's iCloud settings.

6. 儿童隐私

吨吨不会主动收集儿童的个人信息。由于数据默认保存在本地，开发者无法识别用户年龄，也不会建立用户档案。

6. Children's privacy

DunDun does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because data is stored locally by default, the developer does not identify users' ages or create user profiles.

7. 政策更新

吨吨可能会不时更新本隐私政策。更新内容会发布在本页面，并注明新的生效日期。

7. Changes to this policy

DunDun may update this Privacy Policy may be from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
